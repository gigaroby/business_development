When Gut Feeling Is Not Enough

Hiring people is great. It means your business is growing and you can afford to add new talent to your team. At the same time, it can be a huge pain; You have to spend ages trying to find the right person, when you could really be focusing on your core business instead.

At Method Match we understand that you care about the people you hire. Based on solid behavioral research combined with years of expertise, we created a method that will reduce your insecurity when hiring. Our method specifically targets the personal fit between the candidate and your company. It reveals the real person behind the CV, so you find out about their true nature and inner motivations before spending hours interviewing them.
